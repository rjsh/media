443/**

448 * @GNUTLS_CERT_SIGNATURE_FAILURE: The signature verification failed.

462 * @GNUTLS_CERT_EXPIRED: The certificate has expired.
463 * @GNUTLS_CERT_REVOCATION_DATA_SUPERSEDED: The revocation data are old and have been superseded.
464 * @GNUTLS_CERT_REVOCATION_DATA_ISSUED_IN_FUTURE: The revocation data have a future issue date.
465 * @GNUTLS_CERT_UNEXPECTED_OWNER: The owner is not the expected one.
466 * @GNUTLS_CERT_MISMATCH: The certificate presented isn't the expected one (TOFU)
467 *

471 */
